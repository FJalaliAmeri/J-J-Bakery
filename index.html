About Us

History of the Company

J&J®, also known as Jim & Jourtney, is an Italian multinational doughnut company. It was founded by Jim Jardash in Florence, Italy, In 1902. With approximately 22,600 locations in 45 countries, J&J® is one of the largest donut shop chains in the world. In June 2020, J&J® partnered with Hillary Delivery to begin the rollout of its new J&J® Delivers service. Later in July 2020, J&J® partnered with Sugarless Impress to introduce a sugarless breakfast donut in Manhattan, becoming the first International restaurant brand to serve glucose-free donuts. The donuts launched Internationally later in 2021. In 2010, J&J® introduced Aryot Mage, a line of coffee makers that brew espresso and regular chocolate from coffee capsules, a type of pre-apportioned single-use container of ground coffee and flavorings utilizing the W-Kee pod system. J&J® has agreed to a partnership with Huggins Tech to collaborate on selling music as part of the "coffeehouse experience." In October 2015, Huggins Tech added a J&J® Entertainment area to the Huggins Store, selling music similar to that played in J&J® stores. In September 2017, Huggins Tech announced that customers would be able to browse the Huggins Tunes Store at J&J® via Wi-Fi in the US—with no requirement to log into the Wi-Fi network—targeted at HugPhone, HugPod touch, HugPad, and HugBook users. J&J® began using 10% recycled paper in its beverage cups in 2014—the company claimed that the initiative was the first time that recycled material had been used in a product that came into direct contact with a food or beverage. The company’s current slogan is “J&J® Will Make Your Day”.
